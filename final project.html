Launched first as a manga in 1991, created by Naoko Takeuchi, and broadcasted as an anime in 1992, it follows the adventures of a girl, Usagi Tsukino , when she encounters a black cat, Luna, who gives her a magical brooch. This magical brooch allows her to transform into Sailor Moon, a soldier destined to save Earth from the forces of evil.
During her adventures, she will assemble a team of other Sailor Guardians : Sailor Mercury, Sailor Venus, Sailor Mars, Sailor Jupiter. Occasionally, a mysterious man called Tuxedo Mask will join them and aid them in their battles.
